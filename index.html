About Me

I am currently pursing a career in full-stack development. I spent six months at CodeCrew, an intensive coding boot camp upgrading my skills of front and back-end design in a hands-on environment. I love the problem solving aspect of coding that simultaneously requires a big picture and detailed oriented approach.

I have a background in engineering and concern myself with functionality , design and content equally. I look to combine the rationality of code with the subjectivity of design to reach beautiful solutions to complex problems. I was born and brought up in India where I graduated with my engineering degree.

I moved to USA for my Masters in Business with Computer Information Systems. Now that I've spent over 12 years of my life in Memphis this is home. This vibrant city and surrounding towns offer inspiration and support at every turn. I aspire to tap into the pulsing vein of development that runs throughout the city and add to its energy tenfold.
